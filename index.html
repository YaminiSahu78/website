About

I am a PhD Research Scholar in the Department of Computer Science & Engineering at BIT Mesra, Ranchi. I work at the intersection of Healthcare AI and Geo-Informatics, applying Machine Learning and Deep Learning to real-world problems.

I previously worked as a Research Scientist II at AIIMS Raipur (Sept 2024 – July 2026), leading a research team at the Department of Trauma & Emergency on the Intelligent Emergency Healthcare System project — developing critical care tools for remote and underserved hospitals. My core research involved real-time ECG data analysis using deep learning for early detection of cardiovascular diseases, and deploying trained models using MLOps practices into real-time clinical systems.

Earlier, I was a Research Fellow / Doctoral Researcher at IISER Bhopal's Department of Data Science and Engineering (Dec 2021 – Aug 2024), where I contributed to urban planning and GeoInformatics research — including building usage type classification in Indian cities and predicting women's crime hotspots, applying machine learning to spatial and visual data. My work is driven by a strong foundation in Machine Learning and Deep Learning, applied across diverse, real-world challenges.
